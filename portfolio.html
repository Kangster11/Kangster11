Project 3.2.4 Project Honey as an Indicator

The project is about a collected information on bee colony decline. Our goal is to study and extracted information from a large group of data to see if the effect of bee colony health on honey growth. The first bar graph shows the decrease of honey production over the year and the second bar graph shows the numbers of facilities that processes honey in the United States over the year. As a result, the increase in bee colony made the honey production more consistant.
